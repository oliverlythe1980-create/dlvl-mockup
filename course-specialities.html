Specialities & Continuing Education

With a WWII wreck 25 minutes up the coast, walls dropping past 40 metres, and a house reef made for perfecting buoyancy, Amed is where speciality training stops being theoretical.

Pick Your Path

Every speciality runs on the same rules as everything else here: four divers maximum, taught at your pace, all equipment and taxes included. Many more PADI specialities available, if you don’t see yours, ask.

01

Enriched Air (Nitrox)

The most popular speciality in diving: longer bottom times and shorter surface intervals. One day, with or without dives.

IDR 2.150.000theory only · IDR 2.900.000 with 2 dives

02

Deep Diving

Four dives over two to three days, training you calmly down to 40 metres. Amed’s walls make a perfect classroom.

IDR 3.900.0004 dives · age 15+

03

Deep + Nitrox pack

The pairing that makes sense: dive deeper and stay longer. Two specialities in one arc.

IDR 6.400.0002 days · age 15+

04

Ultimate Diver Pack

Advanced Open Water + Deep + Nitrox in three to four days. Arrive an Open Water diver, leave ready for almost anything.

IDR 10.500.0003–4 days · age 15+

05

Wreck Diving

Four dives on the USAT Liberty learning lines, lights and safe penetration limits. The site does the selling.

Ask us4 dives on the Liberty

06

Sidemount

Two tanks, perfect trim, total streamlining. Taught by divers who actually dive sidemount, not just certify it.

Ask usrecreational sidemount

07

ReActivate Refresher

Rusty? A half-day review in the pool, with an optional reef dive to finish. The kindest way back into the water.

IDR 900.000+ 1 reef dive · IDR 1.400.000

08

Emergency First Response

CPR and first aid in one day, for divers, partners, parents, anyone. No dive certification needed.

IDR 2.700.0001 day · open to non-divers
