We Don't Just Dive Here. We Look After Here.

Amed's reefs are our workplace, our classroom, and our home. Conservation isn't a page on our website: it's how every briefing starts and how every dive is led.

01

Reef-first diving

Perfect buoyancy before anything else. Every diver we train leaves knowing how to enjoy a reef without touching it, it's the skill we're proudest to teach.

02

Hands in the water

Reef and beach cleanups, monitoring the sites we dive every day, and raising the alarm when something changes. Ask us what's happening on the reef this season. We'll talk your ear off.

03

Part of Amed

We're a small team rooted in this village, working alongside the local community that depends on a healthy reef as much as we do.
